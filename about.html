About Little Ghost

When was the last time you genuinely connected with something or
someone? Chances are it was far too long ago. We set out to create
a place where friends, family, and strangers could feel good coming
together as a community. With a new setting every season, Little
Ghost invites you to connect with each other over a good cup of
coffee during a once in a lifetime opportunity (literally).
